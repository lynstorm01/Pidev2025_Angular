    As an economic player convinced of the benefits of a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) approach, Maghribia has incorporated CSR as one of its strategic axes. This is in line with its ethical values and the belief in fulfilling a corporate mission that positively impacts society. Maghribia recognizes the need to play an active role in energy transition and environmental preservation.
